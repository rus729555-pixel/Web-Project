Athletica Gym — Project Summary

A responsive multi-page site with a branded header and navbar linking to Home, Products, Facilities, Nutrition, Making of, and Our Story. The first screen prompts login (name + phone), and logging out redirects to register. Membership plans include Basic, Pro, and Premium. Home highlights sample products with a “More” button to the full Products page. There are member reviews for social proof, and the footer includes our social media plus a right-aligned email signup form.
