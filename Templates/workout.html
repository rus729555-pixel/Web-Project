Workouts

Push-Ups

Start in a plank position, lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor, then push back up.

Squats

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your hips down and back, then stand up tall again.

Plank

Hold your body straight in a push-up position, keeping your core tight and your back flat.

Lunges

Step forward with one leg and lower your hips until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.

Crunches

Lie on your back, bend your knees, and lift your upper body towards your knees using your abs.

Bicep Curls

Hold a dumbbell in each hand and curl the weights up while keeping your elbows close to your body. Slowly lower back down.

Shoulder Press

Hold dumbbells at shoulder height and press them upward until your arms are fully extended. Lower slowly to the start position.

Leg Raises

Lie on your back, keep your legs straight, and lift them until they’re vertical. Lower slowly without touching the ground.
